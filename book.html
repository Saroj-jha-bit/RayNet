What is kWh?

In simple terms, 1 kWh is the energy a 1,000-watt appliance uses in one hour. Here are some examples for a typical 2BHK flat:

  • 1 kWh: Can run 2 fans and 4 LED lights for about 5 hours.
  • 2 kWh: Can run 4 fans and 5 LED lights for about 5 hours.
  • 3 kWh: Can run 5 fans and 10 LED lights for about 5 hours.
  • 5 kWh: Can run 8 fans and 44 LED lights for about 5 hours.
